# Mario Kart 3 (Racing Edition)

### Welcome to mario kart 3 racing edition!
To play you need to move a racing car between two lanes.

### How to Play
To play you need to move the racing car left and right using your arrow keys.

### Objective
The objective is to dodge as many cars as you can without crashing into one.

### Rules
* You cannot go out of the road.
* The more cars you dodge the faster it gets.
* If you crash into one of the incoming cars the game will end and you will go to the end screen.
* Have a good time and enjoy the music!
